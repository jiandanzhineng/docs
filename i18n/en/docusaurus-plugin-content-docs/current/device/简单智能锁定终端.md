**Features:**

Connects to Wi-Fi, supports wireless control, terminal lock/unlock, USB Type-C charging.

Hold the button for 60 seconds to force unlock.

Automatic unlock when battery voltage falls below 20%.

Switch direction: Away from the charging port means ON, otherwise OFF.

**Note:**

1. Please keep an escape tool handy during use! Due to network or other issues, the device cannot guarantee unlocking!
