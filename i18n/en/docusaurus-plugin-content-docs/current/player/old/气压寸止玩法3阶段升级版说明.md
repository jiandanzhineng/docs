## Game Flow
The game consists of the following state cycles:

![Flow Diagram](../img/cunzhi3phase.jpg)

1.  **Calm Period (Initial/Sub Calm)**
    -   Vibration intensity slowly increases, accompanied by random fluctuations.
    -   **Goal**: Increase arousal level, raise air pressure.
    -   **Transition**: Enters Middle Period when pressure exceeds "Middle Pressure".

2.  **Middle Period (Middle)**
    -   Intelligent Control: Higher air pressure leads to lower vibration intensity (to prevent too fast climax); Lower air pressure leads to higher vibration intensity (to maintain arousal).
    -   **Transition**:
        -   Air Pressure >= "Critical Pressure": Triggers **electric shock**, enters Edging Period.
        -   Air Pressure < "Middle Pressure": Falls back to Calm Period.

3.  **Edging Period (Edging)**
    -   **Electric shock triggered! Vibration stops.**
    -   **Transition**: After air pressure drops back below the critical value, enters Delay Period.

4.  **Delay Period (Delay)**
    -   Wait for a period of time (cool-down delay).
    -   **Transition**: After the delay ends, returns to Middle Period (if pressure > Middle Pressure) or Calm Period (if pressure < Middle Pressure).

3.  Insert the air pressure device into the body, generally inflate to 20-25 kPa. Ensure the pressure is appropriately low while still having significant change when pressed lightly, which helps slow down air leakage.

4.  Set the Middle Pressure and Critical Pressure. For first-time players, adjustments can be made while playing. Lower these pressures if it gets too unbearable, raise them if it's not stimulating enough.
