## Game Flow
The game consists of the following state cycles:

![Flow Chart](./img/cunzhi3phase.jpg)

1.  **Calm Period (Initial/Sub Calm)**
    *   Vibration intensity slowly increases, accompanied by random disturbances.
    *   **Goal**: Increase excitement level, raise air pressure.
    *   **Transition**: When air pressure exceeds "Middle Pressure", enter the Middle Period.

2.  **Middle Period (Middle)**
    *   Intelligent Control: The higher the air pressure, the lower the vibration intensity (prevents rapid climax); the lower the air pressure, the higher the vibration intensity (maintains excitement).
    *   **Transition**:
        *   Air pressure >= "Critical Pressure": Triggers **electric shock**, enters the Edging Period.
        *   Air pressure < "Middle Pressure": Falls back to the Calm Period.

3.  **Edging Period (Edging)**
    *   **Electric shock triggered**! Vibration stops.
    *   **Transition**: After air pressure drops back below the critical value, enters the Cooling Delay Period.

4.  **Delay Period (Delay)**
    *   Wait for a period of time (cool-down delay).
    *   **Transition**: After the delay ends, determines whether to return to the Middle Period (if greater than Middle Pressure) or the Calm Period (if less than Middle Pressure) based on current air pressure.

## Required Equipment

## Configuration Instructions

| Parameter Name | Description | Recommended Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Game Duration** | Total running time of the game (minutes). | 20 |
| **Critical Pressure** | The maximum air pressure threshold (kPa) for triggering electric shock and CUNZHI. | 23 |
| **TD01 Max Intensity** | Maximum vibration intensity (1-255). | 255 |
| **Middle (Excitement) Pressure** | The pressure threshold (kPa) for entering the intelligent control phase. | 22 |
| **Electric Shock Intensity** | Voltage intensity (V) during punishment. | 20 |
| **Electric Shock Duration** | Punishment duration (seconds). | 3 |
| **Low Pressure Delay** | Cooling rest time (seconds) after CUNZHI is triggered. | 5 |
| **Intensity Increase Rate Limit** | The fastest rate at which vibration increases to avoid sudden changes. | 10 |
| **Intensity Random Disturbance** | Random fluctuation amplitude of vibration intensity (%). | 0 |
| **Gradual Increase After Delay** | The value by which vibration automatically increases per second during the Calm Period. | 10 |
