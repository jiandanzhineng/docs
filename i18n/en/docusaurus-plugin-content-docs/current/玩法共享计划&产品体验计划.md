# Gameplay Sharing Program & Product Experience Program

# Gameplay Sharing Program
Users are welcome to develop their own gameplay based on the device.

After development is complete, you can contact the uploader to list the corresponding gameplay kit. Once sold, the gameplay developer will receive a 5% share of the sales revenue.

For specifics, you can contact the Bilibili uploader, Taobao shop owner, or QQ group administrator.

# Product Experience Program
1. Choose the gameplay you would like to experience.
2. Fill out the Product Experience Officer application form [https://rcnhcfc58y3x.feishu.cn/share/base/form/shrcnE8uStsXM6mJcSL2FPh25mS](https://rcnhcfc58y3x.feishu.cn/share/base/form/shrcnE8uStsXM6mJcSL2FPh25mS).
3. Customer service will contact you if there is a need.
4. Place an order for the product.
5. Record a usage video and send it.
6. The product amount will be refunded to you.

Some notes: No need to expose private areas.
