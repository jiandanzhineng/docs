# Sharing Plan & Product Experience Plan

# Sharing Plan
Users are welcome to develop usage scenarios based on the device.

After development is completed, you can contact the uploader to list the corresponding usage scenario kit. After sales, the scenario developer will receive a 5% share of the sales revenue.

For specifics, you can reach out to the Bilibili uploader, Taobao store owner, or QQ group administrator~



# Product Experience Plan
1. Choose the usage scenario you wish to experience.
2. Fill out the Product Tester Application Form [https://rcnhcfc58y3x.feishu.cn/share/base/form/shrcnE8uStsXM6mJcSL2FPh25mS](https://rcnhcfc58y3x.feishu.cn/share/base/form/shrcnE8uStsXM6mJcSL2FPh25mS)
3. Customer service will contact you if needed.
4. Place an order for the product.
5. Record a usage video and send it.
6. The product amount will be refunded to you~

Some notes: No need to expose private parts.
